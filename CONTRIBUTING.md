# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving these notes. This is a personal, public notes collection, so contributions should aim to keep the content helpful, tidy, and aligned with the overall tone.

## What I Welcome

1. Fixes or clarifications to existing notes.
2. Helpful additions to collections (links, tools, recipes, etc.).
3. New notes that fit the spirit of the vault: practical, concise, and useful to others.

## What I Probably Won't Merge

1. Promotional content or link dumping.
2. Major rewrites that change the original intent of a note.
3. Off-topic content that doesn't fit the vault.

## How To Contribute

1. Fork this repository.
2. Create a branch for your change.
3. Make your edits in Obsidian or your editor of choice.
4. Open a pull request with a short explanation of what you changed and why.
