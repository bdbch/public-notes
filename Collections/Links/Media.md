## Audio

- [Audapolis](https://github.com/audapolis/audapolis) - Open-source audio editor that lets you edit by modifying the transcript (Word-processor for audio).
- [MoisesAI](https://moises.ai/) - Klasse Tool um Audio-Spuren in einem Song zu separieren und potenziell auszublenden.

## Video

- [OpenCut](https://github.com/OpenCut-app/OpenCut) - The open-source CapCut alternative. Free video editing software without watermarks or ads.
- [OpenScreen](https://github.com/siddharthvaddem/openscreen) - Free and open-source screen recorder alternative to Screen Studio with zooms and motion blur.
