This is one for my nerdy hobby. A list of my most favorite Addons for WoW.

## UI

- [ElvUI](https://tukui.org/) - A full on replacement for the whole UI
- [Platynator](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/platynator) - Super simple but still powerful addon for Nameplates

## Cooldown Manager

- [CooldownManagerCentered](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/cooldown-manager-centered) - Adds more options to the Cooldown Manager like centered buttons, action binds on buttons and more

## Bags & Character

- [Baganator](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/baganator) - A bag replacement with categories and a lot of sorting / filtering options

## Other

- [SenseiClassResourceBar](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/senseiclassresourcebar) - Simple and minimalistic resource bars
- [Enhance QoL](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/eqol) - A lot of QoL enhancements, great addon
- [HandyNotes](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes) - Shows notes on the worldmap

## Combat & Raid

- [BigWigs](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/big-wigs) - Boss encounter warnings and timers for raids and dungeons.
- [Clique](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/clique) - Click-to-cast addon for healing.
- [Deadly Boss Mods](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/deadly-boss-mods) - Alerts for boss mechanics, timers, and raid coordination.
- [Details](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/details) - Advanced damage meter with detailed combat analysis and customizable displays.
- [Gladius](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/gladius) - Arena unit frames.
- [LoseControl](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/losecontrol) - Crowd control tracker.
- [NugRunning](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/nugrunning) - Cooldown and buff tracker.
- [Raider.IO](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/raiderio) - Displays Mythic+ scores and raid progress for players.
- [TellMeWhen](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/tellmewhen) - Advanced timer and notification addon.
- [ThreatPlates](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/threat-plates) - Nameplate addon for threat.
- [Unhalted](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/unhalted) - Most popular custom unit frames with plenty of options and customization.

## Professions & Economy

- [Auctionator](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/auctionator) - Enhances the auction house with scanning, pricing, and selling tools.
- [CraftSim](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/craftsim) - Crafting simulation and optimization addon.
- [Fishing Buddy](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/fishing-buddy) - Fishing enhancement.
- [GatherMate2](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/gathermate2) - Gathering profession tracking.
- [Routes](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/routes) - Path optimization for gathering.
- [TradeSkillMaster](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/tradeskillmaster) - Gold making and profession management addon.

## Quests & Exploration

- [AllTheThings](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/all-the-things) - Achievement, mount, and item tracking addon.
- [Angrier World Quests](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/angrier-world-quests) - Lets you filter world quests down to the ones you're interested in.
- [AtlasLoot](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/atlasloot-enhanced) - Loot tables and databases.
- [Carbonite](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/carbonite) - Mapping and questing addon.
- [Questie](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/questie) - Quest helper with maps and notes.
- [RareScanner](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/rarescanner) - Alerts for rare spawns and treasures.
- [TomTom](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/tomtom) - Coordinate navigation and waypoint addon.
- [WorldQuestTracker](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/world-quest-tracker) - Tracks world quests.
- [ZygorGuidesViewer](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/zygorguidesviewer) - In-game guide addon.

## Nameplates & Frames

- [Decursive](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/decursive) - Dispel management for healers.
- [Kui_Nameplates](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/kui-nameplates) - Customizable nameplates.
- [Plater](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/plater-nameplates) - Advanced nameplates with customization for health, auras, and indicators.

## Character Management

- [AzeriteTooltip](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/azerite-tooltip) - Azerite gear information.
- [ItemRack](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/itemrack) - Equipment management.
- [Outfitter](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/outfitter) - Outfit management.
- [Pawn](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/pawn) - Gear optimization tool for comparing and scoring items.

## Reputation & Collections

- [Altoholic](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/altoholic) - Character and guild management.
- [MythicDungeonTools](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/mythic-dungeon-tools) - Planning tool for Mythic+ dungeons with routes and affixes.
- [ParagonReputation](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/paragon-reputation) - Reputation tracker for Paragon factions.

## Utilities & Quality of Life

- [AutoLooter](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/autolooter) - Automatic looting that manages inventory better than default auto loot.
- [BugSack](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/bugsack) - Error collection and reporting.
- [Bartender4](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/bartender4) - Customizable action bars with advanced features for keybinding and layout.
- [Dialogue Key](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/dialogue-key) - Lets you use keyboard shortcuts for dialogue options.
- [Dialogue UI](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/dialogue-ui) - Upgrade to Blizzard's presentation that makes reading quest text way more enjoyable.
- [Leatrix Plus](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/leatrix-plus) - Collection of quality of life improvements.
- [LUI](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/lui) - Full UI overhaul solution for those who want a complete redesign.
- [Masque](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/masque) - Skinning system for action buttons and more.
- [Mount Mog](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/mogmount) - Lets you set up mounts to match each outfit.
- [Opai](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/opai) - Puts non-combat buttons on easy access rings instead of your bars.
- [Plumber](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/plumber) - Adds beautiful UI improvements throughout the game and housing features.
- [SexyMap](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/sexymap) - Minimap customization.
- [Swatter](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/swatter) - Error display addon.
- [Waypoint UI](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/waypoint-ui) - Gives the default map pin a makeover with new features like ETA.
